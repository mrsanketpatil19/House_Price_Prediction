About HousePrice AI

Professional real estate valuation powered by advanced machine learning technology

Our Mission

To provide homeowners with accurate, professional house valuations using cutting-edge AI technology

Objective

Develop a highly accurate machine learning model to predict house prices based on various property features. Our goal is to provide reliable price estimates for real estate valuation using advanced AI techniques.

Dataset

Comprehensive dataset with 1,459 property records and 81 features including location, property characteristics, condition, amenities, and market factors. The data covers diverse property types and market conditions.

Methodology

Applied comprehensive data preprocessing including missing value imputation, outlier detection, feature engineering, and transformation techniques. Multiple regression models were evaluated with cross-validation for optimal performance.

Results

Achieved 87.80% R² score with Ridge Regression, demonstrating excellent predictive power while maintaining model interpretability and generalization capability.
